Pilgrim Tabernacle is currently meeting in a rented portakabin (Martin Primary School) in East Finchley, North London.

Directions

Walk

The location is situated next to East Finchley Library, a 15-20 min walk from East Finchley Station which is on the Northern Line.

Car

We share a parking space with the primary school, which often has available parking spaces during the times that we meet (Sundays, and Tuesday & Thursday evenings). There are no parking costs.

Public Transport

Train – As mentioned above, the location is a 20 min walk from the East Finchley Station which operates via the Northern Line.

Bus – The bus that arrives at the location is the 263. If you are coming from the direction of East Finchley Station take the 263 going to Barnet Hospital and alight at East Finchley library and cross the road over to the primary school.
If coming from North Finchley, take the 263 going to Highbury Barn and alight at Chandos Road, and walk briefly ahead to the primary school.
There are other buses that operate near the area – 102, 234 and 232. But please bear in mind these buses will only get you near the location, so you would have to walk the rest of the journey or take the 263 to the stops mentioned.
